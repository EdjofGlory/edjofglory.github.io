WarScape

WarScape is a First-Person Shooter set in a Dystopian Version of The Philippines. The Game was developed as a requirement for our Main Thesis Project. The game was developed with use of Unity Engine and captilizes the rendering power of the engine. Developed by " Team BITS", A team that consists of Edjeil Dominic B. Labra, Vicylyn Tumaneng and Havey Kyle Unso. I served as the game's Leader and Creative Director, responsible in developing the game's story, gameplay systems and scripting.

The game was received well by our Peers and Professors and has earned a grade of 96% from the defense panel and our Thesis Supervisor. The Game can be freely played and downloaded here
